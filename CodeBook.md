        Time-series  
    f  
        Fast-Fourier Transform

        Body measurement  
    Gravity  
        Gravity measurement

        Linear acceleration  
    Gyro  
        Angular velocity  
    Jerk  
        Time-derived Jerk signal  
    Mag  
        Magnitude calculated using the Euclidean norm  
    mean()  
        Mean of the values  
    std()  
        Standard deviation of the values  
    X  
        X-axis component  
    Y  
        Y-axis component  
    Z  
        Z-axis component
